2. BD Metrics — Definitions & Formulas

Annual ROI (core BD metric)

Annual ROI = (GP90 × 4) ÷ Annual Cost
Where: GP90 = sum of Oct+Nov+Dec+Jan gross profit
Annual Cost = Base + Taxes + Bonus (from SPL)

Lifetime ROI (3x confirmed multiplier)

Lifetime ROI = GP90 × 3 ÷ Annual Cost
Context: avg account lifetime GP ≈ 3× the first 90-day GP
Lifetime ROI ≥ 1.0x = rep has paid back their annual cost from launched accounts alone

GP365 (Annualised GP)

GP365 = GP90 × 4
Used as: revenue run-rate, BoB proxy for BD reps, basis for Annual ROI

Breakeven (new joiners only)

Breakeven (months) = Annual Cost ÷ (GP90 ÷ 3)
= months of GP90-pace output needed to cover full annual cost
Only computed for reps with estimated tenure < 36 months
Only shown at country level when ≥ 3 such reps exist

Account Churn (country-level signal)

Acct Churn % = (reps with GP_Oct > 0 AND GP_Jan = 0) ÷ (reps with GP_Oct > 0) × 100
Interpretation: % of BD-opened accounts that dropped to zero GP by January
This is a COMBINED signal — reflects BD account quality AND MD retention effectiveness

Revenue Freshness

Revenue Freshness % = Total BD GP90 (country) ÷ M1 VFM (country, $) × 100
High freshness = market driven by new account opening (hunter-led)
Low freshness = mature farming market (MD-led revenue base)
Optimal range depends on market maturity; compare vs INTL average

3. MD Metrics — Definitions & Formulas

BoB (Book of Business)

BoB = GP90 × 4 = annualised GP managed by the MD
This represents the total annual revenue value the MD is responsible for retaining and growing

BoB / Live Accounts per Rep (key capacity metric)

BoB per Live Account = BoB ÷ Number of Active Accounts
High value = MD manages large/complex accounts (capacity risk if churn rises)
Low value = MD manages many small accounts (efficiency question)

BoB / Cost Coverage Ratio

Coverage Ratio = BoB ÷ Annual MD Cost
Interpretation: how much revenue each $ of MD salary is protecting
≥ 15x = excellent · 8–15x = solid · < 8x = review capacity or BoB allocation

5. Country Score Calculation

Country Score (0–100) = ROI Score + Growth Score + Churn Score + VFM/FTE Score + Breakeven Score*

ROI Score = min(30, avg_roi × 12) → max 30 pts
Growth Score = min(25, (YoY+0.2)/0.6 × 25) → max 25 pts
Churn Score = min(20, (20−churn%)/20 × 20) → max 20 pts
VFM/FTE Score= min(15, vfm_k / 12) → max 15 pts
BE Score = min(10, (15−avg_be)/10 × 10) → max 10 pts (*only if ≥3 new joiners)

Score 70–100 = strong market | 50–69 = developing | 35–49 = watch | <35 = review

BD Hire Signal Logic

HIRE = avg_roi > INTL_median_roi AND bd_churn ≤ INTL_median_churn AND avg_be < INTL_median_be (if ≥3 joiners)
MAINTAIN = avg_roi > INTL_median AND (churn OR breakeven condition not met)
WATCH = avg_roi ≤ INTL_median AND avg_roi ≥ 1.0x
REVIEW = avg_roi < 1.0x OR churn > 25%

MD Hire Signal Logic

HIRE MD = avg_churn > INTL_md_median AND avg_ggr < INTL_ggr_median AND avg_bob_per_rep > INTL_bob_median
DEVELOP = one metric below median (not all three)
MAINTAIN = churn ≤ median AND growth ≥ median
REVIEW MD = churn > median AND growth < median AND bob_per_rep < median (performance, not capacity)
